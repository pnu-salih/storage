Welcome to the Food Storage & Recipe Management System

Our system is designed to help households and small businesses efficiently manage their food inventory, reduce food waste, and explore delicious recipes based on available ingredients and dietary preferences. Take control of your food and waste today!

What We Do

Our Food Storage & Recipe Management System is an innovative tool for individuals and small businesses alike. We offer a comprehensive platform to track inventory, manage expiration dates, discover recipes, and reduce food waste—all while supporting health and dietary goals.

Inventory Management

Track the freshness of your ingredients, manage quantities, and get notified when items are close to expiring. Perfect for both individuals and small businesses!

Recipe Recommendations

Discover delicious recipes based on your available ingredients, dietary needs, and personal preferences. Healthier eating, made easy.

Waste Reduction

Track food waste and optimize your inventory management to reduce unnecessary waste. Save money, time, and the environment.

How It Works

The system is powered by a relational database consisting of multiple tables, allowing seamless interaction between inventory, recipes, user preferences, suppliers, and waste tracking. Each table is interrelated, ensuring the data flows efficiently to create an intelligent and personalized experience for all users.
